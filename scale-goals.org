* Goals for Scales and Arpeggios
** Phrasing
 - Notes per bow
 - Direction
** Technique (Body awareness)
 - Pull from back
 - Elbow heights
 - Contact point
 - Free weight of arm
 - Curved left fingers
 - Loose fingers
** Rhythm
 - Subdivisions of 2 beats (2, 3..16)
** Intonation
 - Drones
 - Double stops
 - Shifts
 - Paw exercise
** Tone
 - Smooth note transitions
 - Bowings and articulations
 - Bow changes smooth
 - Richness of tone
